Resources for Intermediates

Bodybuilding Style Workout

Once you have laid your foundation and are comfortable with most exercises, if your goal is increasing your muscle mass, this is a great template routine to get you used to the increased volume

Like before, the first number refers to the numbers of sets, the second number refers to the number of repititions

Day 1 - Back, Biceps, and Shoulders

  • Seated Supported Machine Row 4x15
  • Seated Side Laterals 5x20
  • Standing Barbell Curl 4x15
  • One-Arm Dumbbell Row 5x20
  • Reverse Pec-Fly 4x12
  • Two-Arm Machine Curl 4x12
  • Seated Cable Row (close grip) 4x15
  • Seated Dumbbell Front Raise 3x15

Day 2 - Chest and Triceps

  • Flat Barbell Bench Press 4x12
  • Machine Pec Fly 4x20
  • Incline Dumbbell Press 5x15
  • Inclune Dumbbell Pec Fly 3x15
  • One-Arm Alternating Cable Pressdown 4x15
  • Reverse Grip Triceps Cable Pushdown 4x15
  • Incline Skull-Crushers 3x20

Day 3 - Calves, Hamstrings, and Quads

  • Lying Leg Curls 5x12
  • Squats 4x20
  • Standing Calve Raise 3x15
  • Seated Leg Curl 4x15
  • Walking Lunges 4/15 per leg
  • Leg Extension 4x20
  • Seated Calve Raise 3x20
  • tiff-leg Deadlift 3x15
  • One-Leg Leg Press 3x20 per leg
