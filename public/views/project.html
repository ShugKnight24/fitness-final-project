Loving your FitBit?

You can take it to the next level with FitKnit - an interactive app to increase your motivation to get and stay fit.

How it Works

  • FitKnit knits together your fitbit statistics and motivational psychology.

  • Self-monitoring: viewing your activity
  • Cognitive restructuring: positive self-talk
  • Modeling: learning by watching another person perform the healthy behaviors you aspire to
  • Goal-setting: developing concrete, specific steps to take toward a goal
