Why FitKnit?

Loving your fitbit? Unwilling to move if you're not wearing it? You can take it to the next level with FitKnit - an interactive app to increase your motivation to get and stay fit.

How it Works

FitKnit knits together your fitbit statistics and motivational psychology, primarily:

  • Self-monitoring: viewing how many steps you're taking in an average day
  • Cognitive restructuring: self-talk in the style of: "I can do this!" instead of "wow, I'm sick of exercising, and I feel like a big blob and all my steps are between the couch and the bathroom."
  • Modeling: learning by watching another person perform the healthy behaviors you aspire to
  • Goal-setting - developing concrete, specific steps to take toward a goal
